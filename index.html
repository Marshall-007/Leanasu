Welcome to Leannasu Sitho Construction

Empowering Communities Through Quality Construction

At Leannasu Sitho Construction and Projects (Pty) Ltd, we bring over two decades of experience and dedication to the construction industry. We are driven by our commitment to deliver excellence in every project we undertake.

Our journey began in 2019 when our director, who had a successful partnership in a West Rand Furniture Manufacturing Company, recognized the rapidly growing demand for housing. Since then, we have evolved into a trusted name in construction, renowned for our expertise, innovation, and quality workmanship.

Frequently Asked Questions

You May Ask

How can I request a quote for my project?
Requesting a quote is easy! Just visit our Contact Us page and fill out the form with the necessary details about your project. We'll get back to you with a quote as soon as possible.
What types of construction projects do you handle?
We handle a wide range of construction projects including residential, commercial, industrial, and institutional. Our expertise covers everything from new construction to renovation and remodeling.
How experienced is your team?
Our team brings over 25 years of collective experience in the construction and design industry. We have a diverse and skilled team of architects, engineers, and craftsmen to ensure quality work.
How do I schedule a consultation?
Scheduling a consultation is simple. Just give us a call or send us an email with your preferred date and time. We'll confirm the appointment and discuss your project details during the consultation.
What is the typical timeline for a construction project?
The timeline for a construction project can vary based on its size and complexity. We will provide you with a detailed project schedule during the planning phase to give you a clear understanding of the timeline.
Do you provide interior design services?
Yes, we offer comprehensive interior design services to enhance the aesthetics and functionality of your space. Our team will work closely with you to bring your design vision to life.
Can I see examples of your past projects?
Certainly! We have a portfolio of our past projects available on our website. You can explore different types of projects we've completed to get a sense of our work quality and design expertise.
What materials do you use for construction?
We prioritize using high-quality materials that are durable and sustainable. The materials we use depend on the specific project requirements, and we'll ensure to choose materials that best match your needs.
How do you ensure the safety of your construction sites?
Safety is our top priority. We strictly adhere to safety protocols and regulations to create a secure working environment. Our team is trained to follow safety guidelines and precautions at all times.
What sets your company apart from others?
Our company stands out due to our extensive experience, skilled team, commitment to quality, and client-centric approach. We aim to exceed expectations and deliver results that reflect our passion for construction and design.
